32Days Post-Op

Goals

  • FWB walking unaided with normal gait
  • Swelling should be eliminated
  • Full knee extension should be achieved
  • Start knee full ROM mob ex

Precautions & ROM Limits

  • Squats: Knee Flexion < 45°
  • Leg Press: Knee Flexion 0-45°

Outcome Measures / Assessment

Tests/Checks required this timeframe:

  • Check: Full Knee Extension
  • Check: Swelling (should be eliminated)
  • Observation: Normal Gait

Interventions

  • Off crutches for isolated ACL reconstruction
  • Gait re-education +/- treadmill
  • 1/4 partial squats with hand support (knee F <45°)
  • Balance exercise on wobble board
  • Stationary cycling in high-sitting
  • Assisted hamstrings curls in prone for HS
  • Start hamstrings strengthening for BPB
  • Isometric quads at knee F 90° and 60°
  • Close kinetic chain double-leg press at F 0-45°
